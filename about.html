Hover Wheels

What do we do?

We create small-scale hover cars that you can control.

How do they work?

By using Magnetic Field Architecture (MFA).

The cars use 4 electromagnets on the base of the car that uses the earths magnetic field to propel itself off the ground. Allowing the car to hover.
